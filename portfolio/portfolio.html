Top Fertilizer Companies in India

IIFCO

IFFCO is the biggest fertilizer company in India,serving millions of farmers across the country. It manufactures a wide range of fertilizers, including urea, DAP, NPK, and complex fertilizers. IFFCO’s commitment to sustainable agriculture and farmer-centric initiatives has made it a trusted name in the industry Established 1967 Premier Products Urea, DAP, Complex fertilizers Revenue 60,000 Cr. Headquarters New Delhi

CHAMBAL FERTILIZERS

Chambal Fertilizers -is among the largest fertilizer companies in India, part of the KK Birla Group, is a significant player in the fertilizer sector. It produces urea, diammonium phosphate (DAP), and other complex fertilizers. Chambal Fertilizers focus on adopting advanced technologies and sustainable practices to enhance agricultural productivity.Leaving its mark by contributing to 13% of all the Urea produced in the country.Established 1985 Premier Products NPK simple and complex Fertilizers Revenue 16000 CR Market Capitalization 11000 CR Headquarters New Delhi

COROMANDEL INTERNATIONAL

Coromandel International, a subsidiary of the Murugappa Group, is a leading private-sector fertilizer company in India. It manufactures a wide range of fertilizers and agrochemicals, including phosphatic fertilizers, crop protection products, and specialty nutrients. The company’s commitment to innovation and quality has earned it a strong market presence.Established 1961 Premier Products Godavari Ultra DAP, GROMOR Urea Revenue 13000 Cr. Market Capitalization 28000 Cr. Headquarters Secunderabad , Telangana

KRIBHCO

KRIBHCO is another prominent cooperative fertilizer company that caters to the needs of Indian farmers. It produces urea, ammonia, and complex fertilizers, focusing on sustainable and eco-friendly practices. KRIBHCO also emphasizes farmer education and extension programs to enhance agricultural productivity.Established 1980 Premier Products Urea, ammonia, Complex Fertilizers Revenue 13000 CR Headquarters Noida, Uttar Pradesh

RCFLTD

RCF is a PSU engaged in the manufacturing and marketing of fertilizers and industrial chemicals. It produces urea, complex fertilizers, and micro-nutrients, catering to the diverse needs of Indian agriculture. RCF’s focus on sustainable growth and environmental stewardship sets it apart in the industry.Established 1978 Premier Products Bharat Urea, Bharat NPK 15:15:15 Revenue 12000 CR Market Capitalization 5800 CR Headquarters Mumbai,Maharashtra

GSFC

Gujarat State Fertilizers & Chemicals Ltd (GSFC) is a state-owned Urea company in India based in Gujarat.The company has modern manufacturing facilities in Vadodara and serves customers in Gujarat and neighboring states. GSFC Emphasizes research and development, sustainability, and corporate social responsibility.Established 1962 Premier Products Urea, Ammonium Sulfate, NPK blends Revenue 9200 CR Market Capitalization 6300 CR Headquarters Vadodara, Gujarat
